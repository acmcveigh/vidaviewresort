Our Story

A hidden gem
on the McKenzie

Tucked between ancient Douglas firs at Milepost 36.5 on the McKenzie Highway, Vida View Resort offers something rare: two completely private properties where the river is your constant companion — halfway between Eugene and Bend, completely apart from the world.

The four-bedroom Bed & Breakfast sits elevated above the river with sweeping views, a pool, sauna, and designer suites. The Riverhouse rests on the lower riverside — right next to the water, with 750 feet of private frontage and a hot tub perched over the current.

On the McKenzie

Life lived
at river pace

The McKenzie is one of Oregon's most celebrated rivers — cold, clear, and rushing through old-growth forest. Your stay is a front-row seat to all of it.

🛶

Whitewater Rafting Adventures

Experience the McKenzie River's legendary whitewater with an expert guide. From gentle floats to thrilling Class III rapids — all skill levels welcome, all memories guaranteed.

🎣

Fly Fishing

Step off the bank into some of the finest fly fishing in the Pacific Northwest. Native rainbow and cutthroat trout await in our 750 feet of private river frontage.

🏓

Covered Pickleball

Three premium covered courts mean your game goes on rain or shine — this is Oregon, after all. Paddles and balls provided. Bragging rights are on you.

McKenzie River Rafting

Ride the Wild McKenzie

The McKenzie offers some of the most beautiful whitewater in Oregon — emerald water, towering firs, and exhilarating rapids. We've partnered with local outfitters to bring the full river experience to your stay.

Complimentary

River Float Orientation

A short guided float on the gentle section directly behind the property — complimentary for all guests. The perfect way to meet the McKenzie.

Premium Add-On

Full-Day Whitewater Adventure

Half or full-day guided whitewater rafting with a catered riverside lunch. Navigate the McKenzie's legendary rapids with a professional guide who knows every bend.

Premium Add-On

Sunset Drift & Dine

A leisurely evening float timed to the golden hour, followed by a chef-prepared dinner on the riverbank. An evening you'll carry with you long after you leave.
