Tucked between ancient Douglas firs at Milepost 36.5 on the McKenzie Highway, Vida View Resort is the only McKenzie River vacation rental where guided whitewater rafting is included with every stay — halfway between Eugene and Bend, completely apart from the world.

The Bed & Breakfast is a private four-bedroom retreat with a heated pool, Finnish sauna, and designer suites overlooking the river. The River Front House sits right at the water's edge — 750 feet of private river frontage, a hot tub overlooking the river, and your own McKenzie River boat launch.

at river pace

The McKenzie is one of Oregon's most celebrated rivers — cold, clear, and rushing through old-growth forest. Your stay is a front-row seat to all of it.

🛶

Whitewater Rafting Adventures

Experience the McKenzie River's legendary whitewater with an expert guide. From gentle floats to thrilling Class III rapids — all skill levels welcome, all memories guaranteed.

Fly Fishing

Step off the bank into some of the finest fly fishing in the Pacific Northwest. Native rainbow and cutthroat trout await in our 750 feet of private river frontage.

Covered Pickleball

Three premium covered courts mean your game goes on rain or shine — this is Oregon, after all. Paddles and balls provided. Bragging rights are on you.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

Does Vida View Resort include whitewater rafting?

Yes. Every overnight stay at Vida View Resort includes a complimentary short introductory guided river float on the gentle section of the McKenzie directly behind the property. You can upgrade for a fee to the full river experience — more time on the water, a stop at our jump-off rock, and a BBQ lunch served at our private riverside boat launch. A sunset drift-and-dine package is also available.

Where can I stay on the McKenzie River in Oregon?

Vida View Resort offers two private, gated retreats directly on the McKenzie River in Vida, Oregon — a luxury four-suite Bed & Breakfast and The River Front House, with 750 feet of private river frontage. It's one of the few McKenzie River stays that includes a complimentary guided rafting experience with every booking.

What is included with a stay at Vida View Resort?

Every stay includes a complimentary introductory guided river float, standup paddleboards with life jackets, and inner tubes for floating the river — all included at no extra charge. You also get a heated pool, Finnish sauna, riverside hot tubs, pickleball courts, full kitchens, a bar & lounge, and 750 feet of private river frontage. The grounds are private and gated.

How far is Vida View Resort from Eugene, Oregon?

Vida View Resort is about 30 miles (roughly a 40-minute drive) east of Eugene and Springfield, along the McKenzie Highway (OR-126) at 49885 McKenzie Hwy, Vida, OR 97488. Eugene Airport (EUG) is about an hour away.

Can you host weddings at Vida View Resort?

Yes. Vida View is a private riverfront wedding and event venue accommodating everything from intimate elopements to full celebrations of up to 100 guests, with nine bedrooms across both properties so your wedding party can stay on the river all weekend.

How many bedrooms does Vida View Resort have?

Vida View Resort has nine bedrooms total across two private properties — the Riverview Bed & Breakfast with four designer suites, and The River Front House on the lower riverside. Properties can be booked individually or together.
