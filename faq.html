Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your McKenzie River stay — where we are, what's included, how the complimentary rafting works, and how to book.

Location & Getting Here

Where is Vida View Resort located?

Vida View Resort is at 49885 McKenzie Highway, Vida, Oregon 97488 — Milepost 36.5 on Highway 126, directly on the McKenzie River. The property sits in the heart of the McKenzie River corridor east of Eugene and Springfield, with the highway tracing the river for most of the approach.

How far is Vida View Resort from Eugene, Oregon?

About 30 miles east of Eugene and Springfield — roughly a 40-minute drive along the McKenzie Highway (OR-126). Eugene Airport (EUG) is about an hour away, making it the closest commercial airport to the resort.

How far is the resort from Bend, Oregon?

Bend is roughly a two-hour scenic drive east via Highway 126 and the Santiam Pass (US-20) — one of Oregon's most beautiful mountain routes. Vida View makes a natural riverside base for exploring the corridor between Eugene and Central Oregon.

How close is the resort to Blue Pool (Tamolitch Falls)?

The Tamolitch Blue Pool trailhead is about 40 minutes east of the resort along Highway 126, near Trail Bridge Reservoir. Sahalie and Koosah Falls and Clear Lake are a short drive beyond it. See our complete Blue Pool hiking guide for the full plan.

Are there hot springs near the resort?

Yes. Belknap Hot Springs is about 20–25 minutes east on Highway 126, and Terwilliger (Cougar) Hot Springs is roughly 45 minutes away near Cougar Reservoir. Our things to do guide covers both — and back at the resort you can finish the day in the Finnish sauna or a riverside hot tub.

The Properties

What properties can I book at Vida View Resort?

Two private, gated retreats on the McKenzie River: the Riverview Bed & Breakfast — four designer suites with a heated pool, Finnish sauna, and river views — and The River Front House at the water's edge, with 750 feet of private river frontage, a riverside hot tub, and a private boat launch. They can be booked individually or together.

How many bedrooms does Vida View Resort have?

Nine bedrooms in total across the two properties — the four-suite Bed & Breakfast on the upper tier and The River Front House at the water's edge. Booking both puts the entire riverbank under one reservation for reunions, retreats, and weddings.

Is the property really private?

Yes. Both retreats are fully gated with exclusive use of the grounds during your stay — no shared paths, no day visitors, and 750 feet of McKenzie River frontage that belongs to your group alone.

Can I rent a single room, or do I book a whole house?

The Bed & Breakfast rents by the room, so couples can book a single designer suite. The River Front House books as a whole house. Current rates and availability are on the booking page, or call 541-896-1196.

What's Included

What is included with every stay?

Every three-night stay includes standup paddleboards with life jackets, inner tubes for floating the river, and a complimentary introductory guided river float. Guests also enjoy the heated pool, Finnish sauna, riverside hot tubs, covered pickleball courts, full kitchens, free Wi-Fi, and free parking.

Is the guided rafting really free?

Yes. Every three-night stay includes a complimentary introductory guided river float on the gentle stretch of the McKenzie directly behind the property, launched from the resort's own boat launch. Paid upgrades add the full river run with a jump-off rock stop and BBQ lunch, or a sunset drift-and-dine. Details on the rafting page.

Is the pool heated year-round?

Yes. The heated outdoor pool at the Bed & Breakfast is available to guests in every season — the McKenzie runs glacially cold, so the pool, sauna, and hot tubs are a welcome contrast all year.

Can I fish at Vida View Resort?

Yes. The water directly in front of the resort is one of the middle McKenzie's premium trout stretches, known for native rainbow and cutthroat trout. An Oregon fishing license is required, and experienced local guides operate throughout the Vida and McKenzie Bridge area. Start with our complete McKenzie River guide.

Weddings & Events

Can Vida View Resort host weddings and events?

Yes. Vida View is a private riverfront wedding venue hosting everything from intimate elopements to celebrations of up to 100 guests, with the ceremony set against the McKenzie River. See the weddings page for details.

Can our wedding party stay on site?

Yes — with nine bedrooms across the two properties, your wedding party can stay on the river all weekend and have the entire gated grounds to themselves.

Booking & Reviews

How do I book a stay?

Book direct through vidaviewresort.com or by calling 541-896-1196, or reserve through the resort's Airbnb and VRBO listings. For weddings, group takeovers, and booking both properties together, calling direct is the fastest route.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes. Vida View Resort gift certificates are available in any amount from $50 up, never expire, and can be applied to any stay in any season.

Where can I read guest reviews?

Guest reviews of Vida View Resort are on Google, Airbnb, VRBO, and TripAdvisor. The resort holds a 5.0 rating from its guests.
