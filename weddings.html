Your most beautiful day, by the river

There is something a ballroom can never manufacture: the sound of a living river, light moving through old-growth fir, and the feeling of being genuinely, completely away. Vida View Resort is a private riverfront wedding venue on Oregon's McKenzie River — booked on an exclusive-use basis, so the entire gated property belongs to you and your guests.

Whether you're planning a two-person elopement at sunrise or a 100-guest celebration that runs all weekend, the river is your backdrop, your soundtrack, and your witness.

Good to Know

Wedding questions

How many guests can the venue hold?+

From two-person elopements to celebrations of up to 100 guests on the private riverfront grounds.

Is lodging available on site?+

Yes — nine bedrooms across two properties, the four-suite Bed & Breakfast and The River Front House, so the wedding party can stay on the river all weekend when both are booked together.

Is the venue private and exclusive-use?+

Yes. The grounds are fully gated and booked exclusively, so you and your guests have the entire riverfront property to yourselves.

Can we hold both the ceremony and reception here?+

Yes. The riverfront lawn hosts ceremonies, with space across the grounds for receptions and dinners, including covered areas in case of weather.

Where is the venue and what's the nearest airport?+

49885 McKenzie Hwy, Vida, OR 97488 — about 40 minutes east of Eugene on OR-126. Eugene Airport (EUG) is roughly an hour away.
