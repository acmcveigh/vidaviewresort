Good to Know

Rafting questions

Is the guided rafting really free?+

Yes. Every three-night stay includes a complimentary introductory guided river float, launched from the resort's own private boat launch. Longer full-river runs and a sunset float-and-dine are available as paid upgrades.

Do I need rafting experience?+

No. The introductory and scenic floats are designed for complete beginners and families. Your guide handles navigation and covers everything before launch.

When is rafting available?+

Generally late spring through early fall. Summer offers the most comfortable conditions; spring brings higher water, and fall brings golden light and fewer crowds. See our seasonal guide.

What should I wear and bring?+

Quick-dry layers (no cotton), secure river footwear, sunscreen and sunglasses with a strap, and a change of dry clothes. The water is cold year-round, so in spring and fall a light wetsuit helps. Life jackets and equipment are provided.
